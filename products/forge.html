Forge

Early access v0.1.5

What is Forge?

Forge is a desktop application that eliminates the friction of setting up and maintaining automation engines. Instead of SSH sessions, Docker commands, and YAML files, you get a clean interface to install, start, stop, and configure n8n, Node-RED, Kestra and Activepieces in seconds.

🚀

One-click install

Any engine running in under 60 seconds.

📦

156 workflow templates

Ready-to-import automations for common tasks.

🔒

100% local

No cloud, no telemetry. Everything runs on your machine.

🎯

Multi-engine

Run n8n, Node-RED and Kestra side by side.

How to use

1

Download Forge

Grab the installer for your OS from the download section below. Windows, macOS and Linux are all supported.

2

Choose an engine

Pick n8n, Node-RED or Kestra from the dashboard and click Install. Forge handles Docker, ports and configuration automatically.

3

Import a template

Browse the 156 built-in workflow templates and import any of them into your engine in one click.

4

Activate your license

After purchase you'll receive a license key by email. Enter it in Forge to unlock the full version. Valid on up to 3 machines.
